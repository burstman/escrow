FAQ

Common questions.

How does the blockchain part actually work? +

When a contract is created, funds are deposited into a smart contract. Release requires both client and freelancer signatures. Every action is publicly verifiable.

What payment methods are supported? +

Local bank transfer, card, and stablecoins. The on-chain layer settles in stablecoin so value stays predictable.

What if the client refuses to release the funds? +

Open a dispute. Both parties submit evidence and a neutral reviewer decides based on the original contract terms.

Who can use Escrow.tn? +

Any verified Tunisian freelancer and any client — local or international — who wants protected payments.
